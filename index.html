Impact of COVID-19 on Global GDPs

The COVID-19 pandemic, which emerged in 2019 and spread globally in 2020, resulted in a wave of financial crises worldwide. This study aims to analyze the impact of COVID-19 on the GDPs of different countries. The dataset used for this analysis was obtained from Kaggle. The study aggregates data on HDI, total cases, total deaths, stringency index, and population to assess the impact on GDP. The top 20 countries most affected by COVID-19 in terms of total cases are identified and their pre and during COVID-19 GDP per capita figures are included. The findings shed light on the significant economic consequences of the pandemic.

Forecasting SpaceX Falcon 9 Launch Success

This project utilized EDA, interactive Folium, and Plotly Dash to predict the success of SpaceX Falcon 9 launches. Four classification models (Logistic Regression, SVM, Decision Tree, KNN) were built for future launch predictions. The models considered various factors such as launch date, payload type, and mission outcome. The project incorporated interactive Folium maps and a Plotly Dash application for real-time predictions and visualizations. The insights gained enable informed decision-making and risk mitigation strategies, supporting SpaceX in optimizing their launch strategies for mission success.
